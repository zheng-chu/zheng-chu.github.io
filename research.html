Research

Currently, Dr. Chu is mainly working on intelligent sensing and communications, especially in the following research interests:

  • 5G/6G communications and Internet of things (IoT) networks

  • Rydberg atomic receiver and polarforming antenna

  • Smart radio environments/Reconfigurable intelligent surface

  • Integrated communications and sensing

  • Space-air-ground-sea integrated networks (SAGSIN)

  • Smart mobility and transportations

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) driven future networks

  • Wireless powered communication networks

  • Multiple-input multiple-output (MIMO) systems

  • Wireless information security

  • Edge computing and wireless caching
